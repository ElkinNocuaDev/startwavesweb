How can I send money with StartPay?

Sending money with StartPay is easy and secure. Just follow these steps:

1. Download StartPay or visit StartPay Web.
2. Open the app and select the cryptocurrency you want to start your wallet with.
3. Copy and save your security phrase in a safe place.

Once your wallet is set up, you can make transfers quickly and with minimal costs.

What security measures does StartPay have to protect my transactions?

At StartPay, we ensure maximum security in every transaction with advanced technologies:

End-to-end encryption – All information and funds are protected with advanced encryption.
Encrypted funds – Your money is protected with blockchain security systems, ensuring secure and unalterable transactions.
Advanced security protocols – We comply with the highest standards in the financial sector.
Real-time monitoring – We detect and prevent suspicious activities to keep your funds safe.

With StartPay, every transaction is secure, fast, and reliable.
